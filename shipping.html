Shipping Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday to Friday). Once your order is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.

Shipping Time & Delivery

Orders are shipped from our fulfillment center in the United States. Estimated delivery times:

  • United States: 5–10 business days
  • International: 7–21 business days (depending on customs and local carriers)

Shipping Methods

We use reliable carriers such as USPS, UPS, and DHL. Shipping options may vary based on destination.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can use this link to follow your shipment status until it reaches your door.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs (if applicable) are calculated at checkout and may vary based on your location and selected package. Some offers may include free shipping within the U.S.

Delays & Exceptions

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, delays may occur due to factors beyond our control such as weather, customs, or carrier issues. We appreciate your understanding and patience.
